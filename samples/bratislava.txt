Key to map:

# Lines starting and ending with asterisks are the markers for the section describing each street, with the street name between the asterisks.

# Lines beginning with hashes are comments, which are unformatted explanatory text.  There is a comment at the start of each street, indicating which direction it is being described from, and there are some other comments.

# Down the middle of each section there are vertical bar characters representing the street itself.  At junctions, these are replaced by plus signs, and at pedestrian crossings, they are replaced by percent signs.

# Side streets are marked with three dashes, a space, the side street name, a space, three dashes.  They are followed or preceded by a plus sign marking the street being described.
